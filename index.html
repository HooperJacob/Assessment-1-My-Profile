My interest started in technology started when my nan got me a GameBoy Pocket for Christmas as a kid, and my passion for all things tech blossomed from there.

A recent experience that sparked my interest in helping other people while in my role as an Assistant in Nursing, in Aged Care. I found that I really enjoyed helping people understand the new technology in the workplace through using terminology they understood in order to do the documentation digitally.
